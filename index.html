Scenic Route Feature

Scenic Route Feature for Google maps offers a route where the time value is combined with fun, and possibility to see the scene: a picturesque stop on the way or a gallery opening.

Beauty Time

The prototype is a suggestion of a microsite for a new product line of a beauty brand "Beauty Time". Technical task from the company is the reference point for creating the prototype. We made style decisions according to the provided brand attributes: "tender, exclusive, stylish".

Moi Svyatiye Sushi

The project is a HiFi prototype for the home page of a sushi delivery service "Мои святые суши". The page includes blocks that are required in the technical task. We made the rest of design decisions after competitors analysis.
